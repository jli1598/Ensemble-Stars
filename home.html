Ensemble Stars! (あんさんぶるスターズ！) is a multimedia project. Originally released as a Japanese mobile card collecting game on May 1st, 2015, it has a novel, a manga, and multiple stage plays released, with an anime scheduled for July 7th, 2019.


Game Sypnosis

Yumenosaki Private Academy is an all-male idol training course that has a long history of producing talented idols. You are a second-year transfer student at this school, and you have been chosen to be the very first student of the brand-new producer course! Your task is to produce these idols as they work through their tumultuous pasts, create new bonds, and become part of a bright, resounding ensemble!
